Scott Jordan

Principal | Founder | CEO

Scott, founder of Arrived, has been an active investor in real estate since 2016 and has experience performing underwriting and due diligence on $500M+ acquisitions. Scott has experience analyzing financials for $60B divisions at a top 10 fortune company. Scott has managed opening, setup and operations of 40+ short-term rental units. Scott completed his education at the Marriott School of Business with a BS in Strategic Management. 

Tanner Hale

Principal | Co-Founder | CIO

Tanner, Co-founder of Arrived, has performed in the top 1% in sales production year over year for a sales team of over 6,000+ reps. Tanner brings experience from working for the largest commercial real estate services and investment firm in the world. Tanner is a devout real estate investor in residential opportunities and has overseen and successfully managed 15+ short term rental properties. Tanner graduated with honors in the Business Management program from Brigham Young University-Idaho.

Maxx Johnson

Principal | Co-Founder | COO

Max, Co-founder of Arrived worked at Deutsche Bank on the FX Derivatives Trading Desk after studying mathematics with a probability emphasis at Brigham Young University. He has worked for two Venture Capital Funds as well as running a few one-off tech start-up businesses. Max oversees Arrived Partners STR management operations and technology development including regular interface with our off-shores management team in Guatemala and the Philippines.
